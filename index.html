Do It For The Kids is a non-profit foundation focused on empowering underprivileged children. The foundation runs initiatives and partnerships to provide essential resources and support for the next generation. With a focus on education and community involvement, we strive to make a positive impact in the lives of young people.

DIFTK

In addition to these efforts, Do It For The Kids is committed to community involvement, encouraging people to get involved in the cause and work together to make a difference. Through partnerships with companies, friends, and organizations, the foundation runs initiatives and programs that inspire and engage people to give back to children in need.

DIFTK

The aim of the Do It For The Kids foundation is to make a positive impact in the lives of underprivileged children. The foundation seeks to achieve this aim through a range of initiatives and partnerships, with a particular focus on education and community involvement.

DIFTK

One of the primary goals of the foundation is to provide essential resources and support to children in need, such as school uniforms, textbooks, and other educational supplies. The foundation also works to provide access to education and learning opportunities, which can help break the cycle of poverty and provide children with the tools they need to succeed.

Who We Are

Do It For The Kids is a non-profit foundation founded in October 2022 by a trio of passionate individuals - Jenifer, Phumla and Lutho - who have all personally experienced the hardships of poverty. Their experiences inspired them to take action and make a positive impact in the lives of underprivileged children, providing them with hope and opportunities for a brighter future.romantro velumde

Company Values

"Do It For The Kids" is committed to empowering underprivileged kids in need by providing essential resources and support

Values

Empathy: We believe in the power of empathy to connect with and understand the needs of underprivileged children.

Equity: We believe in equity and strive to ensure that all children have equal access to the resources and support they need to thrive.

Values

Empowerment: We are dedicated to empowering underprivileged children to overcome challenges and succeed in their education and future endeavors.

Sustainability: We believe in creating long-lasting and sustainable solutions that will continue to benefit underprivileged children for years to come.

Values

Community involvement: We recognize that working with the community is essential to achieving our mission, and we prioritize community involvement as a core value.

Collaboration:We recognize the importance of collaboration in achieving our mission, and we prioritize working with other organizations.

Values

Education:Education is at the heart of our foundation, and we prioritize providing essential resources and support to ensure that all children have access to quality education.

Impact:We are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of young people and strive to ensure that our efforts lead to tangible and meaningful outcomes.
